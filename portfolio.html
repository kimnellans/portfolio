The Challenge: Nourishing Local Communities

Addressing Accessibility and Mobility Issues

Understanding the People

The primary patrons of Locavore are people who value fresh, local produce and the farmers who supply it. To delve into the needs and challenges of these distinct groups, comprehensive research was conducted, encompassing surveys and interviews.

Patrons: The research focused on understanding their shopping habits, motivations, and challenges in buying local produce, along with their comfort level with digital platforms. The insights revealed a strong desire for fresh local produce but highlighted the need for a people-friendly digital solution.

Farmers: For farmers, the study centered on their current inventory management methods, challenges in reaching patrons, and comfort with digital platforms. The need for an efficient way to communicate available products to customers was a key insight.
